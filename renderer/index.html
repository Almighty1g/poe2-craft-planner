About this build: the crafting logic (every orb & omen behavior + the recommended sequence) is built for patch 0.5.x. The mod data — 61 gear bases with their real prefixes/suffixes (spawn weights, tiers, tags, Essence mappings) plus the 200+ desecrated mods (Amanamu/Ulaman/Kurgal) — is generated from Craft of Exile's 0.5 dataset, so the mod lists and odds are live-patch accurate. Odds shown are first-order (weight ÷ side pool); real crafting also blocks already-present mods and group conflicts, so treat them as a strong guide.
